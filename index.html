FAQs

Is this a real therapist?

No, it's an AI trained by top therapists to emulate their style. It's not a replacement for medical advice but can be incredibly helpful for support and reflection.

How do you train the AI?

We work with licensed therapists who contribute anonymized session data, theory frameworks, and behavioral models to create highly accurate AI avatars.

Yes. Privacy and confidentiality are top priorities. Sessions are encrypted, and we never share your data without consent.
